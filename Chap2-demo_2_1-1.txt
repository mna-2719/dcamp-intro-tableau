Sorting and excluding multiple fields

In the preceding demo, you learned how to filter through selection and how sort the order of data in your visualization. It's time for you to test out these skills.

    Load the 2_1_Sorting_and_excluding_multiple_fields.twbx workbook (located in the Workbooks folder on the Desktop).
    Exclude the years 2006 and 2007.
    Sort Country in alphabetical order.
    Keep only the first 10 countries that are listed.
    Sort by Cell Phones per 100 people in descending order.

After these actions, which country is listed first?

Possible Answers

    Uruguay
    Zimbabwe
    Antigua and Barbuda
    Kiribati
