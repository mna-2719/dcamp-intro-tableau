Calculated field for rounding

There's no need to memorize all of the functions because of Tableau's handy built-in documentation.

    Load the 2_5_Calculated_field_for_rounding.twbx workbook (located in the Workbooks folder on the Desktop).
    Create a new calculated field called Rounded Women 25-34.
    Use the built-in documentation and look up ROUND().
    When defining Rounded Women 25-34, use Round() to round the column Women 25-34 to 1 decimal point.
    Replace Women 25-34 in the Marks card with your newly created field.

With the use of filtering, you should be able to answer the following question.


How many countries in 1976 have a value of 8 for Rounded Women 25-34?
2
