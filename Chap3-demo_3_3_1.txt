Your data by year

Now it's your turn! Your task is to analyze mortality in Singapore over the past 40 years. The World Health Organization is interested in knowing how the number of deaths has been evolving. 

To build this line chart you need to:

    Load the 3_7_Your_data_by_year.twbx workbook (located in the Workbooks folder on the Desktop).
    Create a line chart with the date (in years) as columns and the total number of deaths as rows.
    Filter the data on Country to only include Portugal.
